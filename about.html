Description:

EcoTrail connects nature lovers and volunteer opportunities to help maintain these beauties. Enjoy different adventures with your family and friends and meet new people.

Mission: Increase outdoor exploration and make nature accessible to people of all ages and abilities.

EcoTrail - Where every hike makes a difference.

FAQs

What is the purpose of this organization?

To show off the beauties of Cape Town and allow people to volunteer to help around different trails and views.

How can I get involved?

Enter your name and email, as well as a message.

What are the volunteer requirements?

Someone who is willing to be helpful.
